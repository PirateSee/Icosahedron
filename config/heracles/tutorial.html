Tutorial

Quests are a way to guide you through the progression. They will help you learn how mods work and point out things you might miss.


Quests are split into groups. Each group has a set of quests that you can complete. You must complete a quest's dependencies first before being able to finish it.


Some quests may grant rewards for completion.

Go to the "Planetfall" quest to claim your starting equipment.
